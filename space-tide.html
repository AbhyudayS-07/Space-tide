  1. Words should have easy formation.

  3. Words should be easy to pronounce.

    I → me   |   We → mip

    Examples:
    My → mir

    Examples:
    Water → driker

    Travel → bage

    Water → driker

    Examples:
    Today → now
    Tomorrow → noil

    Examples:

    Light → suni

    Live → liv
